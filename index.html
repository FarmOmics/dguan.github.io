Dailu Guan received a Bachelor degree in Animal Science and Master degree in Animal Genetics and Breeding from Southwest University. Then, He received Ph.D. in Animal Genomics from Autonomous University of Barcelona in 2020. He joined Huaijun Zhou group in 2021 as a postdoc, where he was trained in computational biology. His work focused on dissecting genetic architecture of complex traits via integrating multi-omics data..

Research interests

My research focuses on integrating multi-omics data to understand the genetic architecture of complex traits. I have developed pipelines for analyzing multi-omics data, including whole genome sequence (WGS), RNA-seq, ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq, RRBS, WGBS, Hi-C data. Through integrating multi-omics data, I try to how genomic variants determine phenotypic diversity.
